PHASE.01 [ notion ]

THERE’S SOMETHING BEYOND THE OVERTLY DIGITAL.

In our lifetime, there are time periods that we affectionately reminisce for. There are emotional forces pulling us towards certain memories.

This is something virtually everyone experiences at some point in their lifetime. A sentimental desire to re-experience past events.

Why do we find our past perspectives worth re-experiencing? What drives us to consume & create novel experiences? Why do we find value in art?

PHASE.02 [ perception ]

A LOVE FOR WORLD BUILDING. A LOVE FOR CREATION. A LOVE FOR RESPONSIBILITY.

There is a lot of value in these memories we yearn for. These recollections allow us to give context for what we encounter in the present.

This concept is most commonly known as our points of reference. We use previously developed points of reference to contextualize what we are experiencing in the present.

Having a well defined point of reference allows us to discern between quality, authenticity, importance, and value.

PHASE.03 [ articulation ]

WORLDVIEW.

What happens when our points of reference dictate our present motivation to seek and create new and impactful experiences?

Can our previous experiences project themselves onto new experiences?

How can we distill these experiences into our guiding worldview?
